What us crazy people do at SP Gaming Shop

We go about our business just like everyone else does. It just so happens that our business is the gaming kind. We are gamers ourselves, and we know the struggles of spending the money on the games, the consoles, the accessories and the collectible items due to them being expenseive. We take that into consideration and slash those prices with a sword to make everything affordable for the average joe.

Our Gameplan is You, the Gamers Out There

We make our shop a place to hangout - the new after school hot spot. As gamers ourselves, we wish we had a cool place growing up to go to and talk about and play games other than our living rooms. We like to give off a good vibe and have some fun while in our shop.

Oh yeah, it's that good. See for yourself.
